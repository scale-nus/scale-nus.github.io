Welcome Prof. Yuhan Liu (HIT) for a research visit

Armin Lederer, Lei Zheng, Zhihua Wu, Tharusha Fonseka
Jul 1, 2026 1 min read News

We warmly welcome Prof. Yuhan Liu, Associate Professor from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), for a research visit to SCALE in July 2026.

Armin Lederer, Lei Zheng, Zhihua Wu, and Tharusha Fonseka look forward to meeting with Prof. Liu to discuss learning-based control, robotics, and safe autonomy.

Learn more about Prof. Liu at https://homepage.hit.edu.cn/liuyuhan?lang=en.
